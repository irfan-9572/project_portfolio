Mushroom classification using machine learning technology

In the Mushroom Classification for Edibility project,I have develop a robust machine learning model to classify mushrooms as either edible or poisonous based on their features. The dataset employed for this task is a supervised dataset containing various attributes of mushrooms, such as cap shape, cap color, odor, and more. The primary objective was to build a model capable of accurately predicting the edibility of mushrooms, thus serving as a valuable tool for mushroom enthusiasts and foragers.
